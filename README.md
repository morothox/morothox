# morothox

> High school student. Triathlete. I train models — and I train for races.

I spend most of my time in two places: a terminal and a race course. Both taught me the
same thing — the numbers don't lie, and progress is just the sum of days you showed up.

---

## What I'm about

**Data is home base.** I like pulling messy datasets apart — cleaning, exploring, and
questioning them until they actually say something — then turning that into a chart or a
model that holds up under scrutiny.

**Machine learning is the current climb.** I'm working through the classical models and the
math underneath them *before* reaching for the big frameworks. Fundamentals first.

**Triathlon keeps me honest.** Swim, bike, run. I log my own training telemetry and analyze
it myself — that habit is honestly where most of my curiosity started.

**I stay close to the metal.** I write C and C++ to understand what happens beneath the
abstractions, and I daily-drive Arch Linux for the same reason.
